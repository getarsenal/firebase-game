FIREBASE — FIELD MANUAL
MISSION
Enemy mechanized columns are advancing on FOB ALPHA along a single road. Deploy howitzers, dig in, and call indirect fire to stop them before they breach the wire. You have limited supply — spend wisely. The campaign runs 6 groups of progressively harder waves; survive all of them to complete the deployment.
DIFFICULTY
Pick your tier on the start screen. Difficulty affects starting supply, enemy HP, spawn pacing, and reward multipliers.
· RECRUIT — Easier enemies, more starting supply. Career stats still record.
· STANDARD — Baseline balance. Recommended first run.
· VETERAN — Tougher enemies, +10% reward, less starting supply.
· SPARTAN — Punishing. +25% reward. For experienced commanders.
GAME SPEED
Tap the speed button (lower-right of the map) to cycle 1× → 2× → 3×. Higher speed runs the simulation faster — useful for clearing later waves once you have a stable defense set up. Speed is preserved across waves until you change it.
SUPPLY
Supply is your currency. Earn it by destroying enemies and from passive income during active waves. Spend it on guns, FDC/Observer units, crew resupply, repairs, fortifications, and one-time ammo activations. Running dry leaves you unable to reinforce — plan ahead.
HOWITZERS
Tap any slot ring on the map to open the gun picker. Gun slots are always visible as dashed amber rings — they grow brighter when you're in placement mode. Hold-press a slot to deploy the selected gun. Each gun has DAMAGE, RELOAD, and RANGE stats that improve with kills (rank up). Five weapon systems unlock progressively:
· M119A3 — Light, cheap, fast reload. Good for early groups.
· M777 — Balanced towed howitzer with crew MG guard.
· M109A7 — Self-propelled, medium range, high damage.
· M270 MLRS — Rocket artillery, long range, area effect.
· M142 HIMARS — Maximum range and damage. Unlocks late.
GUN INFO PANEL
Tap any placed gun to open its info card. From here you can:
· REPAIR — Restore HP to full (costs supply, between waves only for towed guns).
· DISPLACE — Remove the gun and recover part of its cost.
· CREW — Resupply wounded/killed crew members (towed guns only).
· DIG IN — Build progressive fortifications (Scrape → Sandbag → Hardened). Fortifications absorb incoming fire before the gun takes damage.
· CAMO NETTING — Reduces counterfire chance after digging in.
· FIRE MODE — Switch between Direct and Indirect fire. Indirect requires FDC coverage and fires on an arcing trajectory over terrain.
· AMMO — Select a special ammunition type for this gun (see AMMUNITION below).
AMMUNITION
Each gun fires army-green HE shells by default at no cost. Special ammo types unlock by group and require a one-time activation payment. Once activated, any eligible gun can load that ammo type for free for the rest of the run.
· HE — Standard. Free. All platforms.
· Canister — Anti-personnel shotgun blast. $200 activation. Group 2. Light howitzers.
· Smoke — Slows enemies 50% in impact zone. $250 activation. Group 2. Indirect only.
· WP — Burns enemies in zone for 6 sec. $400 activation. Group 2. All platforms.
· Illumination — Spots all enemies in wide radius, no FO needed. $300 activation. Group 3. Indirect only.
· RAP — +30% range, no damage penalty. $350 activation. Group 3. M777/M109.
· DPICM — Cluster munition, 5 submunitions. $600 activation. Group 3. Indirect only.
· Copperhead/Excalibur — Zero CEP guided round. $800 activation. Group 4. Light howitzers.
· GMLRS — Zero CEP guided rocket. $800 activation. Group 4. Rocket systems.
· Dragon's Breath — Close-range shockwave, 4× damage. $500 activation. Group 4. Direct only.
· ATACMS — Ballistic missile, massive splash. $1500 activation. Group 5. M142 only.
CREW & MG GUARD
Towed guns (M119, M777) have crew members that animate around the gun. One crew member is the MG Guard — they auto-engage enemy infantry and light vehicles within short range. Wounded crew reduces effectiveness. Crew can be resupplied between waves.
FDC — FIRE DIRECTION CENTER
The FDC unit extends the effective range of all guns within its coverage radius and enables Indirect Fire mode. Place it centrally to cover multiple guns. It is a high-value target — enemy infantry will prioritize attacking it. Costs $280. Place from the unit tray at the bottom of the screen.
FORWARD OBSERVER
The Forward Observer watches a sector of the enemy route. Any enemy in the FO's arc is spotted for indirect fire — guns in indirect mode can engage spotted targets beyond normal range. Costs $160. Place from the unit tray.
FIRE MISSION
When you accumulate Fire Mission tokens (earned by group completion), the [FM] button activates in the side menu. Tap it to arm, then tap anywhere on the map to call a precision artillery strike. Tokens carry over between waves.
FORTIFICATIONS
Use DIG IN from the gun info panel during the break between waves. Three stages: Scrape In (basic cover), Sandbag Wall (medium protection), Hardened Post (heavy protection). Each stage increases the gun's HP pool. Fortifications absorb all damage first — the gun itself stays safe until the emplacement is destroyed.
UPGRADES
After completing each group, spend supply on permanent upgrades that apply to all weapons for the next group. Upgrades include extended range, faster reload, harder emplacements, Steel Rain splash damage, and more.
GUNNER VIEW (3D)
Tap a placed gun's GUNNER VIEW button to drop into a first-person 3D view from that gun's optic. The terrain is rendered at scale from the actual USGS topography of Everett, PA. You'll see friendly guns firing alongside you, enemy units approaching along the road, and your shells arcing toward the target. Pinch/drag to look around. Tap EXIT to return to the tactical 2D map. Use this to scout terrain, watch a key engagement, or just enjoy the show.
S2 INTEL REPORT
After each non-boss wave, the wave-complete screen shows an S2 INTEL REPORT briefing on the upcoming wave: a stencil top line summarizing the threat type, the disposition (counts of each enemy type), and tactical hints based on what's coming. Use the briefing to choose ammo loadout and gun placement before tapping START.
CITATIONS (ACHIEVEMENTS)
Twelve citations to earn across kill counts, campaign progression, weapon mastery, and defense streaks. Check Menu → Citations to see your progress. Notable awards include UNTOUCHED (clear a wave with zero FOB damage), STOIC (complete a group with no gun losses), and SPOTLESS RECORD (complete the full campaign without losing a single gun). Unlocked citations persist across runs.
CAREER STATS
Menu → Career Stats shows lifetime totals across all runs: total kills, kills by enemy type, kills by gun, runs completed, longest run, and more. Career stats persist forever unless you reset.
ENEMY TYPES
· Scout (UAZ-469) — Fast, light, low HP. High reward.
· Truck (Ural-4320) — Slow convoy truck. May carry infantry (Group 2+).
· BTR-80 / BMP-2 — Armored personnel carriers. Medium armor. Return fire at short range.
· T-72 / T-80 — Main battle tanks. Heavy armor, high HP. Flash white on hit. Return fire.
· MSTA-S SPG — Self-propelled howitzer. Counter-battery fire at your guns from long range.
· UAS Drone — Fast air target, swarms in large groups. M119 with proximity fire is most effective.
· TOS-1A — Boss unit. Fires thermobaric rockets at your guns from extreme range.
ENEMY INFANTRY
Some trucks (Group 2+) carry dismounted infantry. The first cannon hit on a troop carrier deploys 4 soldiers who charge the nearest gun, FDC, or FO. Infantry bypass armor and deal melee damage directly. Only MG guards, FO-spotted indirect fire, and Canister/Dragon's Breath rounds engage them — standard cannon HE will not target infantry. The S2 Intel Report flags upcoming waves with troop carriers.
RANK SYSTEM
Each gun earns kills. Accumulate enough kills to rank up from PVT to SGM. Higher rank = more damage, faster reload, longer range. Rank is lost if you displace the gun.
SPATIAL AUDIO
Sound volume reflects your camera position and zoom. Zoom into an active gun to hear it fire up close. Zoom out and the battle sounds distant. Pan to isolate sounds across the map.
CONTROLS
· Pan — Drag one finger across the map.
· Zoom — Pinch with two fingers.
· Select/Open — Tap a gun or unit.
· Place unit — Tap a slot ring to open picker, hold-press to deploy.
· Camera reset — Menu → Reset View.
· Pause — Tap the FIREBASE logo in the HUD.
· Speed — Tap the 1× / 2× / 3× button on the lower-right.
